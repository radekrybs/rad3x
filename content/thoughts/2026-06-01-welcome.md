I've wanted a single place to put things for a long time — not a feed owned by
someone else, not a platform that reshuffles itself every few months, just a
plain page I control.

So here it is. The plan is simple:

- **Thoughts** for whatever I'm chewing on.
- **Ventures** for the things I'm building.
- **Analysis** for the longer, more careful write-ups.
- **Knowledge** for the references I want to keep around and share.

## Why write at all

Writing forces the fuzzy stuff in my head to become specific. If I can't explain
an idea in a few paragraphs, I probably don't understand it yet. Publishing adds
a little healthy pressure to get it right.

I don't expect every post to be polished. Some will be half-formed. That's the
point of a personal site — it can hold the rough drafts alongside the finished
work.

> The best time to start writing was years ago. The second best time is today.
