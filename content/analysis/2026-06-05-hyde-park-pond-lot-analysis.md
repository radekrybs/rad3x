This analysis pairs two interactive models for the Pond Lot site.

The **warm-shell feasibility model** runs a full development pro forma for a
two-storey medical building: adjust land price, construction cost, rent,
building size, financing, hold period, and cap rate, and watch financing
viability, the equity gap to a bankable DSCR, sources & uses, a year-by-year
pro forma, the cash-flow chart, and exit returns recompute live.

The **land-valuation dashboard** values the parcel from the ground up — a mapped
set of comparable land sales and active listings, a sortable/filterable comp
table, valuation charts by tier and year, and a zoning-impact analysis that ties
the supportable price to the parcel's likely zoning, with an opening / target /
walk-away framework.
